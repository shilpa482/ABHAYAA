Number of reported crimes against women across India in 2019

Domestic abuse or cruelty by husband and/or relatives was the highest reported crime against women across India in 2019. Assault ranked second that year, with 88 thousand cases filed under the Protection of Children from Sexual Violence Act ranking third. Rape, which makes headlines regularly in the country recorded over 32 thousand. This was from a total of over 405 thousand cases of crime reported against women that year
